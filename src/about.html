About Me

I'm a senior at Portland State University working toward a B.S. in Computer Science with a minor in Mathematics, graduating Spring 2026. With 3+ years of hands-on experience in IT support, systems administration, and AV deployments, I've built a deep understanding of enterprise environments and what it takes to keep them running reliably.

I've also contributed to blockchain development in the private sector and provided Tier 2/3 support in Linux and Windows environments through the Computer Action Team.

My goal is to move into cybersecurity or software engineering, applying my systems knowledge and technical background to roles that protect and improve the tools people depend on. I'm drawn to mission-driven teams in tech, security, and the sports and entertainment space.
